Walk more


So get up and move throughout the day, taking frequent stretch breaks, choosing the stairs over the elevator, and five- to ten-minute walks throughout the day. The need to move our bodies has been gradually eroded from our modern lives and has been replaced with the modern concept of ‘exercise’. But, if we can incorporate regular movement into our daily lives, we will feel the profound benefits. Movement helps to reduce stress, improve our mood and so much more. We can reap the many benefits and movement does not have to be difficult, it can be something you love already such as walking, dancing or even playing.

Benefits of TRX suspension training

According to a recent study regular participation in TRX suspension training could positively alter the risk factors for many major cardiovascular diseases, including muscle fitness, body fat, and decreased waist circumference.

  • TRX can be used by anyone - no matter your fitness level.
  • Burn more calories and get a stronger core and more muscle mass.
  • Workouts can be done anywhere.
  • Easy to get toned using TRX.

Older adults can try new exercises with TRX as they are more stabler. As TRX offers a dynamic point of stability.
