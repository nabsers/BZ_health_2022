Bluezone Lifestyle

The term "bluezone" describes the environments and the lifestyles of the world's longest-living people. These blue zones are scattered across the world with communities of individuals with much longer lifespan than any other areas in the world. Some of the most well-known bluezone regions include, Sardinia-Italy, Okinawa-Japan, Icaira-Greece, Nicoya-Costa Rica, and Loma Linda-CA, USA. With bluezone lifestyle, your journey begins for a less stressed, happier life.
