Eat a Rainbow

Aim to eat at least five portions of vegetables everyday. Preferably five different colours.

While eating more vegetables and fruit is always a good idea, focusing on eating a variety of colors will increase your intake of different nutrients to benefit various areas of your health.

  • Eating all the colours of the rainbow can help boost brain health and reduce the risk of heart disease.
  • Decreases inflammation in the body and helps to prevent cancer.
  • Keeps your immune system strong and healthy.
  • Keeps your bones, teeth and nails strong.

Each color represents a different phytochemical and set of nutrients that may benefit your health.
